Policy

Guiding decisions with data.

AAAS CONGRESSIONAL SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY POLICY FELLOW

I am currently a AAAS Congressional Science and Technology Policy Fellow sponsored by the Soil Science Society of America where she works with the Senate Committee on Agriculture, Nutrition, and Forestry. As a committee staffer, Dunivin works on projects related to climate change, precision agriculture, pesticides, and forestry.

CHRISTINE MIRZAYAN SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY POLICY FELLOW

As a Mirzayan Science and Technology Policy Fellow, I worked with the National Academies of Sciences, Engineering, and Medicine to launch the Environmental Health Matters Initiative. I helped prepare background materials on environmental health topics including plastics, water infrastructure, PFAS, and agriculture. I also worked with the Board on Chemical Sciences and Technology.

SCIENCE DEBATE - LOCAL CHAPTER FOUNDER

I co-founded the first local Science Debate chapter where I advocated for public discussion of science policy topics, including food, water, climate change, cyber security, and mental health.
